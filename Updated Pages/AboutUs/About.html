About Us

We are a group of developers who are currently in college and we aim to create a local platform where artists are able to share their arts to the public and also earn commissions from the request of the people. We are based from the Philippines and our problem started when we figured that rising artists have been having a hard time to exhibit their skills during this pandemic. We thought of a solution to bring safe and healthy environment to the people who are recently exposed in the art industry of the Philippines. In addition, we aim to monetize their talents through commissions and bring them their target audience.
